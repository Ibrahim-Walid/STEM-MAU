Diverse Committees

Specialized committees focusing on different aspects of African affairs including peace and security, economic development, and social issues. Each committee addresses specific challenges and opportunities facing the continent, allowing delegates to develop expertise in particular policy areas.

Resolution Drafting

Students collaborate to develop comprehensive resolutions addressing pressing challenges facing the African continent. Through careful research and negotiation, delegates craft policy documents that propose innovative solutions to complex problems, balancing national interests with continental priorities.

Diplomatic Skills

Delegates practice negotiation, public speaking, and strategic thinking in a professional diplomatic environment. These essential skills are developed through structured debates, informal caucuses, and coalition-building exercises that mirror real-world diplomatic processes at the African Union.
