About STEM Model African Union

STEM MAU (Model African Union) is a flagship educational venture formed in Egypt’s STEM Schools aimed at nurturing diplomacy, leadership abilities, and critical thinking skills of outstanding STEM high school students. In an attempt to broaden issue areas for discussion and acceptable differences for different perspectives, we simultaneously strive to understand and solve Africa’s contemporary political, economic, and social problems through diplomacy. STEM MAU simulates the African Union (AU), allowing participants to hold high-level engagements. Students are allocated to different specialized committees where they conduct in-depth research, innovative negotiations, and collaborative interactions in developing solutions to problems using real practices of the African Union. Members of STEM MAU are exposed to intensive training and hands-on experience, in addition to participation in public speaking, negotiation, policymaking, and teamwork, which builds strong and sharpened STEM skills necessary for the conferences. STEM’s mission focuses on creating positive change and impact in the communities requiring its aspects to ignite the new generation while expertly guiding and preparing them to ethically lead, motivated by policies that shape them.
