About STEM Model African Union

Students engaging in a STEM MAU Conference

STEM MAU (Model African Union) is an educational initiative founded in Egypt's STEM Schools to foster diplomacy, leadership, and critical thinking among high school students. It simulates the African Union (AU), engaging students in research, negotiation, and collaborative problem-solving to address Africa's political, economic, and social challenges. Through public speaking, policymaking, and teamwork, STEM MAU equips students with essential skills for leadership and innovation, aiming to inspire the next generation to drive positive change across the continent.

Vision & Mission

At STEM MAU, we are focused on enhancing students' skills through political awareness, public speaking, and diplomacy for the mission and vision. As a student-led organization simulating the structure and function of Model African Union conferences, we offer high school students an opportunity to step into the shoes of politicians, interact, discuss various issues facing the world today, and appreciate the intricacy surrounding regional and international politics.

Through these committees, which are guided by seasoned heads and co-heads, we prepare our members to acquire and refine critical skills such as research, policy formulation, negotiation, and formal debate.

We strive to foster a generation of youth who are well informed, educated, and able to articulate confidently when assuming leadership and changing roles, as our vision explains.

We focus on constructive political processes and African political affairs as well as nurture teamwork, dignity, social politeness, and patriotism. During the entire season, members undergo rigorous training, article revision, and committee work culminating in our signature event, the STEM MAU Conference.

This multidisciplinary conference is our magnum opus, wherein all diplomatic training culminates for participants role-playing various African Union member states involved in sophisticated policy justification wars and structured deal-making during policy-laden diplomacy.

Our Goals

🗣️

Diplomacy

Master negotiation and policy-making in an African context. Students engage in simulated AU committees, debating and crafting resolutions to address real-world issues.

🔬

STEM Innovation

Apply science and technology to solve African challenges. Participants integrate STEM skills to propose innovative solutions for sustainable development.

🌟

Youth Leadership

Build confidence and skills for Africa's future. Through training and hands-on experience, students develop leadership abilities to drive impactful change.
